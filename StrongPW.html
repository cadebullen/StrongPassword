HINT What is a strong password? A strong password:

  • Does not contain words easily found in a dictionary.
  • Contains a varaition of charcters:
    • Uppercase letters.
    • Lowercase letters.
    • Numbers.
    • Special characters.
  • At least 10 characters in length.
  • Does not conform to popular patterns:
    • Initial capital letter.
    • 2-4 digits at the end of the password.
    • Adding an exlcamation point (!) or dollar sign ($) at the end.
  • Password is not similar to other passwords used on current/previous website.
  • Password does not contain your name.
